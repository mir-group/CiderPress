analyzers
=========

The :py:mod:`ciderpress.pyscf.analyzers` module provides
post-processing tools for analyzing PySCF DFT calculations.
In particular, sub-classes of :py:class:`ciderpress.pyscf.analyzers.ElectronAnalyzer`
can generate densities, kinetic energy densities,
exchange energy densities, etc. on a real-space grid.
